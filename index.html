Robot Bounce: A puzzle robot game
How to play:

  Use arrow keys to move the active robot (denoted by text and red arrow) around the board.
  Press TAB, or mouse click on another robot to change the active robot.

  The objective is to move the robot of the color matching the active target, shown in the center, to the target itself.
  The goal is to minimize the number of moves it takes to do this. After reaching the target, you can rewind to try and get a lower score.
